About the project Seeyourseat

View before you book

Have you ever booked a seat in a theater only to find out later that the view wasn't what you expected? Our project solves that problem by giving users a virtual experience of the seat's view before making a reservation. With an immersive 3D interface, you can select any seat in the theater and instantly see the stage from that exact perspective—just like you're sitting there. This feature ensures that you can make informed decisions, avoid poor angles or obstructed views, and enjoy your show to the fullest. Whether you're attending a movie, play, or concert, our platform puts the power of choice and clarity in your hands—before you book.
